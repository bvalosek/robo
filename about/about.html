Credit where credit's due

Several awesome free and open-source pieces of software were used in Robo. A few of these libraries require their license agreements to be included with any derived works‐ a small price to pay... consider it done.

Below is the entire list of third-party code used in Robo. A huge thanks is due to all of the developers that had a part in making this framework come to life.

  • Backbone.js
  • Font Awesome
  • jQuery
  • keymage.js
  • require-less
  • RequireJS
  • Sizzle
  • text
  • Underscore.js

Robo's font is Google's Roboto.

Robo's visual style is inspired heavily by the sexy work being done by the UX/UI people at Microsoft and Google with their latest operating systems.
