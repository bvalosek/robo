Robo was built with several amazing free and open-source libraries. Leveraging the creative talent of the community allowed the creation of this framework to be even more fun and rapid that it already was. A few of these libraries require their license agreements to be included with any derived works‐ a small price to pay... consider it done.

"If I have seen further it is by standing on the shoulders of giants"
